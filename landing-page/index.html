How It Works

Up and running
in one morning

No onboarding calls. No consultants. ReadyRoute is self-serve by design.

01

Connect Your Portal

Set ReadyRoute as a vendor in your dispatch portal's data subscriptions. Your manifest pulls automatically every morning after that.

02

Add Your Drivers

Enter each driver's name and set a 4-digit PIN. They download the app, log in, and their route is waiting.

03

Assign and Push

Match routes to drivers in the manager portal and push to their phones. Routes appear on the driver app immediately.

04

Monitor Live

Watch the live fleet dashboard from anywhere. See every driver's progress in real time — your office, your truck, or your phone.
